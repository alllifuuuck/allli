8. Bryant-Denny Stadium

Bryant-Denny Stadium, Tuscaloosa, AL, United States 101,821 spectators. This stadium is the home stadium for the University of Alabama football team. The stadium opened in 1929 and was originally named Denny Stadium in honor of former University of Alabama president George H. Denny. The stadium name was amended to Bryant Denny Stadium in 1975 after the Alabama legislature chose to honor famed Alabama coach Paul Bear Bryant. The stadium underwent many expansion over the years, most recently in 2010.

9. Darrell K Royal Texas Memorial Stadium

10. Melbourne Cricket Ground

Melbourne Cricket Ground (MCG), Melbourne, Australia 100,018 spectators. Opened in 1854, this multi-purpose stadium is used for cricket, Australian rules football and soccer. It is the home stadium to the Melbourne Cricket Club. It is the largest stadium in Australia and the biggest cricket stadium in the world. The MCG is remembered world wide as the main stadium of the 1956 Summer Olympics.
