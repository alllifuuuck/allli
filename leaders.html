People

1. Muhammad Ali Jinnah

Muhammad Ali Jinnah is considered to be the most influential and greatest leader of all time. That is why he comes at the number 1 spot in the top 10 greatest leaders of all time. Jinnah was a politician of the sub-continent who successfully campaigned for Pakistan. He is known there as Quaid-I Azam for his tremendous amount of work for establishing Pakistan. Today, Jinnah is famous for changing the lives of Muslims in the sub-continent. If it was not for him, Muslims would not have gotten Pakistan. Muhammad Ali Jinnah contributed more than any other man to ensure the survival of Pakistan. Muhammad Ali Jinnah claims the top spot in the list of the top 10 greatest leaders of all time.

2. Nelson Mandela

Nelson Mandela was the first ever President of South Africa that was elected by the use of democratic elections. He was the face and the leader of the Anti- Apartheid movement. Just like Martin Luther, he fought against racism. He served a long prison sentence for his actions but it did not falter him. His focus, determination and will-power were so great that after serving almost 30 years in solitary confinement, he got out and worked for the needs of the people of South Africa.

3. Mahatma Gandhi

Mahatma Gandhi led India against the horrifying rule of the British from Britain. He fought silently and protest against the Britishers by the use of non-violence. He believed that to achieve independence, a non-violent movement should take place in order to protect everyone from harm. Boycotts of foreign goods and Civil disobedience movements etc showed that he was resisting the British rule. After the departure of the British, Gandhi became the main face of the Indian nation.

4. Martin Luther King Jr.

Martin Luther believed that is could make the US a better place. Martin Luther King was an activist of USA as well as the leader of the civil rights movement. He is best remembered for his non-violent protest for the civil rights that started hi civil rights movement. He fought for racial equality and emphasized that every man and women should be treated as equal and should not discriminate each other. He won The Nobel Peace Prize for campaign of bringing peace and establishing unity across all of America.

5. Abraham Lincoln

Abraham Lincoln is best known for signing the Emancipation Proclamation therefore ending slavery in the US. He being the 16th president of the USA was determined to lead the US people to the right way. He also led the civil war in the US. His work is considered to be very important for the people of America as if it was not for him, America would have been a very small country instead of a larger country. His humility, oratory skills and conviction make him the 5th greatest leader in the list.

6. Mao Zedong

Mao Zedong is the one who led the Chinese Revolution and founded the People Republic of China and he is the one who overthrew the Nationalists that controlled China at that time. He withheld the Japanese invasion. He is considered the mind behind the sudden burst to success for China that allowed the nation to become a superpower. His principles of governance and ideologies and economics are now known as Maoism. He is the 6th greatest history leader of all time.
