People

7. Adolf Hitler

Hitler is considered to the most ruthless leader of the world but he should be considered as one of the world greatest leaders. Although he was disliked due to his strict measures but his leadership and military skill are only matched by few people in the world. He became the German chancellor in 1933, and led the Germans through the Great War. Meticulous planning, strategizing and propaganda by Hitler have in some way, shaped the power politics of the world.

8. George Washington

George Washington is best known as the Founder of the USA. He was the powerhouse behind the American Revolution and is known as the First President of the US after its independence. He believed that America had the ability to become one of the great superpowers in the world. His steadfastness, tenacity, his ability to make difficult decisions made him a great leader.

9. Napoleon Bonaparte

Napoleon Bonaparte was a leader in the French military and he is the one person who rose to power after the Great French Revolution in which he was responsible to overthrow the Monarchy. His great knowledge of fighting allowed his small army to triumph against the big armies of the enemies. The Napoleonic Code of Legal Reforms was introduced in the democracy by him and is still applied in some places. His military leadership and the ability to care for the need of every common man have earned him the 9th spot in the list.

10. Franklin D Roosevelt

Franklin Roosevelt held the post of the President of the US during the period known to us as The Great Depression. Roosevelt was confirmed that he had polio at the age of 29 and that paralyzed of both his legs but still he fought polio and successfully became President in 1933. He is the first ever President to be elected four times until his death. He led his fellow Americans to World War II and made America rise to power. His political knowledge allowed him to be featured in the list of the top 10 Greatest Leaders of all time.
