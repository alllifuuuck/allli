Strange TopTens

1. Transparent House, Japan

Inspired by our ancient predecessors, who inhabited trees, this completely transparent (House NA) in Japan offers you a lot of day light, but not much privacy.Designed by: Sou Fujimoto Architects.

2. Skateboard House, USA

The Skateboard House allows you to skate on all the surfaces, both in and outdoors, and was planned to be built in Malibu. Designed by: Pierre Andre Senizergues.

3.The World Slimmest House, Poland

The Keret House, inserted between two existing buildings, measures only from 92 to 152 centimeters in width! Designed by: Jakub Szczesny.

4. Old Water Tower Turned Into Modern Home, Belgium

A 100-feet high water tower in Belgium used to serve as a Nazi hideout during the war, but was later transformed into a living space. Designed by: Bham Design Studio.

5. Dick Clark Flintstones Inspired Home, USA

This single storey house in Malibu was inspired by the Flintstones family from the classic 60 cartoon. Television legend Dick Clark have listed it for $3.5 million.

6.The World Smallest 1sq Meter House, Germany

This portable DIY wooden structure measures only 1 square meter and is the world smallest house, moving around Germany. Designed by: Van Bo Le-Mentzel.
